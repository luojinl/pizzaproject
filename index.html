About Us

Our pizza is crafted with a rich history of passion and

tradition. We use the best ingredients to create a perfect

balance of flavours. Inspired by classic recipes, each pizza

reflects our commitment to quality and the art of pizza-making.

Our History

  • 1995

    We started as a small, family-owned pizzeria. We wanted to create pizzas that brought people together.

  • 2005

    We opened our second location and introduced new pizza varieties, including Margherita and BBQ Chicken, which quickly became fan favorites.

  • 2010

    Our commitment to quality earned us recognition in local and national food awards.

  • 2020

    During challenging times, we shifted focus to delivery, making it easier for our customers to enjoy their favorite pizzas from the comfort of their homes.

  • 2025

    We remain dedicated to quality, tradition, and innovation. Our pizzas continue to bring joy and flavor to generations of pizza lovers.
